All the Maps that are fit to print

What are you doing making maps?

This is a question that I get often.

Personally, maps can help to explain the social and economic contextual nuances in which schools and districts exist. Additionally, I find that making maps offers a creative outlet that is usually not traditionally afforded in the social sciences.

What types of maps do I make?

As an aspiring social scientist, I primarily make thematic maps. However, I enjoy making shaded relief maps in my spare time.

What else do I do?

While Adobe Illustrator is integral to providing the finishing touches to static maps, I really enjoy how it can be used to create fun cartoons. Often times, I will freehand the cartoons then color and stylize them in Illustrator.
